What is Emerging Technology

Emerging technologies are technologies that are largely unrealized that are slowly emerging from obscurity or non-existance

Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality is one of the best examples of emerging technology, VR is a device that can simulate real world activities.

VR in Medicine

VR is on track for wide-spread use in the medical scene. VR worlds can be used to ease your pain or anxiety or to explain a complex medical procedure or condition.
